# Retail_E-Commerce_Data_Analyst
Retail E-Commerce Performance Dashboard
Overview

The Retail E-Commerce Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and monitor key business metrics for an online retail company. The dashboard provides insights into revenue generation, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic sales distribution, enabling data-driven business decisions.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following KPIs:

Total Revenue: 8,887.21K
Total Orders: 19K
Total Customers: 4K
Average Order Value (AOV): 0.48K

These metrics provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

Dashboard Components
1. Monthly Revenue Trend

A line chart that displays revenue performance across different months, helping identify seasonal trends, growth patterns, and sales fluctuations.

2. Revenue by Country

A bar chart showing revenue contributions from different countries. This helps businesses identify top-performing regions and potential markets for expansion.

3. Top 5 Product Revenue

Displays the five highest revenue-generating products, enabling businesses to understand which products contribute most to sales.

4. Revenue by Customer Segment

A pie chart categorizing revenue based on customer segments such as:

High Value Customers
Regular Customers
One-time Customers

This analysis helps improve customer retention and marketing strategies.

5. Top 5 Revenue by Customer ID

Highlights customers generating the highest revenue, allowing businesses to identify valuable customers and build targeted loyalty programs.

Filters Available

The dashboard includes interactive filters for:

Invoice Date
Country
Customer Segment

Users can dynamically filter data and explore insights based on specific business requirements.

Business Benefits

This dashboard helps organizations:

Monitor overall sales performance.
Track revenue growth over time.
Identify best-selling products.
Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
Discover high-value customers.
Compare country-wise sales performance.
Support strategic decision-making with real-time insights.
Dataset Information

The dashboard is built using retail transaction data containing:

Invoice Number
Invoice Date
Customer ID
Product Description
Quantity Sold
Unit Price
Revenue
Country Information

Project Outcome

The Retail E-Commerce Performance Dashboard provides a centralized view of business performance, enabling stakeholders to analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and product profitability efficiently. By leveraging Power BI's interactive capabilities, users can gain actionable insights and make informed business decisions.
